## Fill rules

- **Optional step.** If no trend intersects cleanly with what you do, leave this blank and move to Step 10 — trend alignment is an enhancement, never a prerequisite for success.
- **All five gates must be Yes.** A trend that touches only one or two circles is a mismatch: product-only is irrelevant to your buyers, market-only is hype with nothing behind it, trend-only is pure noise.
- **Declare the market category first — non-negotiable.** Trends are only understood relative to a known market. Lead with the category, then layer the trend (e.g. "a marketplace for pet services… part of the shift toward on-demand, trust-based platforms" — never "the sharing economy for pets").
- **No product link, no trend.** Trend terminology without a concrete product connection is hype that collapses when customers or investors look for substance.
- When in doubt, skip it. Better a little boring than completely baffling.
