## Fill rules

- **One canonical source.** This is the accepted baseline; require all new campaigns, decks, and pages to start from it — do not build copy on top of modified previous copy (that is the drift anti-pattern this document exists to prevent).
- **Don't write messaging in the room.** Agree the sales story arc as a team first; marketing translates it into the wording here independently.
- **Anchor everything to the market category.** Lead with what the product IS before what it does; keep pricing and roadmap language consistent with the declared category so customers don't feel cognitive dissonance.
- **Proof, not adjectives.** Every value-theme message carries at least one objective proof point; the company's own opinion is not proof.
- **Update on reposition.** Any positioning change (especially a market-category shift) means revising this document before new copy ships.
